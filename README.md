## Goal and Purpose
I'm making a website for people with special needs. Low attention span, so we need more sensory stuff and calming help.

Done by the AngryNerds Club (1 member is on github)

## What's included?
* Prototype 1 — sensory toys
* Prototype 2 — pomodoro
* Learning Mode — AI generated unfinished code (I had no time to do this)
* Product — AI generated finished product (but of course, I had to debug this a lot) (I mean, what could I expect for vibe coding more than 200 lines?)

## Different Prototypes
* Prototype 1 has sand, waves, and bubbles. Simplistic and sensory.
* Prototype 2 is a simple pomodoro. Customizable. Black during focus, but goes gradient during break time.
* Our integration idea is to keep focus calming, but introduce toys during break. It includes a stress squishy and different display options for the timer.

### So, what's our final product like?
* Focus mode: black screen. Ring and bar timer display. Stress squishy.
* Break mode: sensory toys. Before 2 sessions, waves. From 2 to 5 sessions, bubbles. After 5 glorious sessions, you can spill the sand.
* Sound plays when mode switches.
